Production Process description Most casting companies in the NYC area use machines (centrifugal, vacuum chamber) for steps 6 and 7 of the lost- wax casting process when casting jewelry or small sculptures. These techniques guarantee clean, detailed casts if set up properly because the pouring method is more reliable. A centrifugal casting machine uses the weight of the metal and centrifugal force to fill the shell. A vacuum casting machine uses a vacuum to suck the molten metal into the shell.
